Your Health Depends on How You Fill Your Plate

Your ability to stay healthy and perform at your best in daily life is greatly influenced by the choices you make at mealtime. What you put on your plate fuels your body, supports your immune system, and provides the energy needed to handle all your activities, from work to exercise to relaxation.

The key is to focus on a balanced diet that includes a variety of nutrients. A plate that is filled with nutrient-dense foods will give you the strength and vitality you need to thrive.

Remember: A healthy plate is a balanced plate!

Tips for a Healthy Plate

  • Half of your plate should be vegetables and fruits: These are rich in vitamins, minerals, and fiber, which are essential for your body's proper functioning.
  • Include lean proteins: Such as chicken, fish, beans, or tofu. Proteins are the building blocks of your muscles and are crucial for recovery and energy.
  • Add whole grains: Whole grains like brown rice, quinoa, and whole wheat bread provide complex carbohydrates that keep you full and energized.
  • Don’t forget healthy fats: Include sources of healthy fats like avocados, nuts, and olive oil to support brain function and overall health.
  • Stay hydrated: Drink plenty of water throughout the day to help your body process nutrients and maintain energy levels.
